Cookbooks series on how to build a widget for Jupyter from scratch
==================================================================

In this series you will find every piece of knowledge necessary to build
a widget for Jupyter Notebook from scratch. In this series, I've integrated React,
but you could use any other JS library or Framework to build the front end part 
and bind it with Python.
